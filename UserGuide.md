## Vehicle Guide

### Powering On the Vehicle

> ⚠️ **WARNING**: When connecting the batteries to the vehicle, ensure that the polarity is correct. There is **NO** reverse polarity protection.

1. Connect two 12V batteries in series to the motors.
2. Connect one 12V battery to the electronics.
3. Turn on the transmitter (see [Transmitter Guide](#transmitter-guide)).
4. Ensure the e-stop button is fully pressed down.
5. Toggle both switches to the **ON** position.
6. Release the e-stop button by twisting it.

---

### Powering Off the Vehicle

1. Activate the e-stop by pressing the e-stop button.
2. Toggle both switches to the **OFF** position.
3. Disconnect all three batteries and remove them from the vehicle.
4. Power off the transmitter.

## Transmitter Guide

### Powering On the Transmitter

Flick the power switch to the **ON** position.

* A startup screen will appear.
* If a warning screen appears about the arming switch, toggle the **top right switch**.

---

### Screen Display

The LCD home screen shows:

* Connection status
* Arming switch status
* Light switch status
* Battery percentage
* Joystick positions

---

### Menu Display

To access the menu:

1. Ensure the robot is **disarmed**.
2. Press the **Menu / Enter** button (top button).

Available options:

* **Auto Calibrate** → Automatically tunes joystick parameters
* **Manual Setup** → Manually configure joystick parameters
* **Sw and Btn Test** → Test switches and buttons
* **Factory Reset** → Restore default joystick settings

Use the **Up/Down** buttons to navigate and press **Enter** to select.
Press the **Back** button to exit.

---

### Driving the Vehicle

1. Ensure you are on the **home screen**.
2. Toggle the **arming switch ON**.

   * A beep indicates the vehicle is armed.
3. Use the joysticks:

   * Left joystick → Left track
   * Right joystick → Right track
4. To stop, toggle the **arming switch OFF**.

---

## Tips & Safety

* If at any point the vehicle behaves erratically, press the e-stop button. **DO NOT** try to stop the vehicle using your body as the motors on the vehicle are of high torque.
* Before operating the Vehicle, ensure the transmitter has enough battery. The battery percentage reading is unreliable.
* Ensure the e-stop button functions properly before operating.
* If there are any connectivity issue, try turning the vehicle and transmitter on and off.
